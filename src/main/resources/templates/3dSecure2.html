Pay with 3DS-2.0

NOTE: You can also do an authorization using 3D Secure 2.0, depending on your merchant settings.

Here are the processes that occur for this transaction:

  1. Use the CREATE_CHECKOUT_SESSION operation to collect sensitive card data.
  2. Initiate Authentication
  3. Authenticate Payer
  4. Use the PROCESS_ACS_RESULT to check if the authentication was successful.
  5. Use session.id and 3DSecureId to complete the transaction using PAY or AUTHORIZE.
